Ectomorphs


An ectopmorph has a lean body type. This ususally consists of having very little body fat but also very little msucle. They have a hard time gaining weight and storing fat. Thye have narrow-chested chest frames and have a hard time bulking up and putting on weight and muscle. Both males and females alike tend to have narrow shoudlers and hips, and typically have fast metabolisms. For males, it is also found that this body type has lower testerone levels than other body types.





Fitness Tips


Since Ectomorphs have a hard time gaining weight, it is advised to avoid cardiovascular workouts such as running and biking if one has the goal of building up muscle and weight. Since cardiovascular exercises expend more energy, they burn more calories and so ectomorphs who already can burn fat quicker than most, should focus on workouts where the amount of weight lifted is prioritized in place of high energy expendeture workouts. Ectomorphs can also take supplements, such as mass gainer, to help with gaining muscle and mass faster. As a reudlt of a higher metabolism combined with working out ectomorphs should eat a higher caloric diet as they tend to burn these faster and need to replenish more often. Endomorphs


The body type of endomorphs are typically opposite of ectomorphs. They have larger bone structures, and tend to gain fat faster and have a harder time losing it. Hence, they tend to carry more fat around and have a stockier frame than ectomorphs, with larger midsections and hips. They can however gain muscle faster than other body types and gain weight without much difficulty. Compared to ectomorphs they have slower metabolisms and should be cautious of this as it can lead to health problems.





Fitness Tips

Incorporating cardio will help this body type burn calories and fat, something that is harder for endomorphs. Interval workouts are also highly encouraged by mixing high-intensity workouts with rest period in between. Studies have shown this will help burn more fat during a workout. Mesomorphs


The body type of mesomorphs tend to be somewhere in the middle of those of endomorphs and ectomorphs. They have medium bone structures, witht he shoulders being a little bit wider than the hips. They can both gain weight and muscles with relative ease, as well as losing fat and muscle. They tnd to have athletic and strong body frames. Their metabolism also tends to be very well as they can eat without as much worry as an endomorph. Their weight also tends to be evenly distributed through their body, so no one area typically stands out.



Fitness Tips

Mesomorphs tend to have an athletic build and a well-balanced body composition. Mesomorphs have a wide array of exercises and workouts they can do and can get results faster than most. HIIT, High-intensity interval training, is one way mesomorphs can maintain that atheletic build while building more muscle at the same time. Mesomorphs are the ideal standard for body types and with the right training and diet, both ectomotphs and endomorphs can transform their bodies to this body type. Mesomorphs should have a healthy balance of both caloric and protein intake. A balanced diet, such as 30% protein, 30% fat and 40% carbohydrate, is one mesomorphs can adopt to keep their workout performance consistent, while also gettin the results they are aiming for.
